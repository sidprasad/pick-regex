Welcome to PICK

PICK helps you guide AI-generated regexes by voting on concrete examples—so you keep control of what ships.

How PICK works

  1. Describe the strings your regex should match.
  2. PICK asks your configured language model for several regex candidates and removes near-duplicates.
  3. We show example strings; you upvote/downvote/skip. Votes eliminate incompatible regexes until one wins—or none fit.

Tip: If nothing fits, revise your description instead of starting over—your past votes carry forward.

Why we show this

  • PICK is research software; expect occasional quirks while we improve it.
  • Bug reports help us fix issues quickly.
  • We love hearing about cases where PICK nailed it or missed—we learn from both.
