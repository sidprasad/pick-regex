Welcome to PICK

PICK helps you shape AI-generated regexes by voting on examples until the best fit emerges.

How PICK works

  1. Describe what the regex should match.
  2. We propose several regex candidates and filter near-duplicates.
  3. You vote on example strings; votes eliminate mismatches until a winner (or none) remains.

Why this notice

PICK is research software, so you may see occasional quirks. We appreciate patience and bug reports. Interesting success or failure cases help us improve.
