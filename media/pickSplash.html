About PICK

PICK helps you build a regex from a natural-language description by iteratively refining it with examples.

You start by describing what the regex should match.

PICK sends that description to an LLM of your choice to surface several plausible interpretations.

It then shows example strings, and your votes narrow down which behavior best matches your intent.

PICK is research software. You may encounter rough edges or unexpected behavior. We appreciate patience, bug reports, and especially concrete cases where the tool either works particularly well or misses the mark—these directly inform our research.
